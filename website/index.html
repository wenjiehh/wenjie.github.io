Intro

Hello! This is Huang Wen-Jie. I graduated from Luodong high school, Yi-lan, and currently am studying International Business at National Taiwan University, which equips me with the ability of logical thinking and the sensitivity toward markets.

Work

I have been an exchange student at Osaka University, Japan for one year. I was working as a translator assistant and proofreader in TEN Co., Ltd., which was a translation company. I am detail-oriented and able to handle multiple tasks. In addition, I have been the head of marketing on the team of TEDxNTHU. My work has involved copywriting, operating Facebook fan page, and organizing multiple activities to advertise our annual event. This experience also makes me a good team player through the cooperation with different groups.

About

I play badmintons, and Tai Tzu Ying is one of my favorite players. Reading novels is also one of my hobbies, and I highly recommend Run with the Wind written by Shion Miura. Besides, listening to music is one of my biggest enjoyment. Feel free to click eight to share the joyful moment with me.
